In a rapidly changing world with increasing competition, our brands represent some of Global Payments' most valuable assets. Our commitment to adapt, grow and lead our customers into the future with technology-enabled, software-driven solutions is stronger than ever, and we need to manage our brands with the same level of commitment.  

To that end, we have developed this Brand Center with a brand architecture that takes into account the many ways we engage customers around the world. The base brand guidelines here will evolve and expand to include the entire Global Payments ecosystem over the coming months, and this will serve as the foundation for our collective brand identity. 

Designed to elevate quality, establish consistency and improve efficiency, the guidelines provide a roadmap for using our brand assets—logotypes, templates, color and typography—so that our brand remains consistent and recognizable across all communications in all markets. 

I am confident the Brand Center will add significant value to our daily operations and customer interactions and will help us tell our story around the world with a unified voice. 

David E. Mangum
President and Chief Operating Officer
